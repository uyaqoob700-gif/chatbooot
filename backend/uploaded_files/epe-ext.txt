Attention Registered Engineers (REs) (Extension of Date for Submission of Applicatons)
Pakistan Engineering Council has reescheduled to hold 25th EPE (Fall-2025) for REs to elevate their title to "Professional Engineer (PE)" on 16th November 2025 (Sunday) instead of 2nd November 2025. The last date of submission of applications has also been extended upto Wednesday, 15th October 2025.
Registered Engineers (REs) fulfilling the above eligibility criteria may apply through the Link (https://cpd.pec.org.pk/EPECustom). Exam format, EPE syllabi, study material and necessary guidelines are available on PEC website under CPD link (https://pec.org.pk/cpd/engineering-practice-examination/).
