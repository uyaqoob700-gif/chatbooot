PEC CHAIRMAN INFORMATION

Current Chairman: Engr. Waseem Nazir

ABOUT THE CHAIRMAN:
Engr. Waseem Nazir serves as the Chairman of Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC). He brings extensive experience in engineering leadership and has been instrumental in advancing the engineering profession in Pakistan.

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Leading Pakistan Engineering Council's strategic initiatives
- Overseeing engineering education and professional development
- Ensuring compliance with engineering standards and regulations
- Promoting engineering excellence and innovation in Pakistan
- Representing Pakistani engineers at national and international forums
